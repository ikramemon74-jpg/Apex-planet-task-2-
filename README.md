
# Apex Planet Internship – Task 2

# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) & Business Intelligence

## Objective
To explore the sales dataset, uncover trends, identify business insights, and answer business questions using SQL and data visualization techniques.

## Tools Used
- Excel
- SQL (SQLite)
- Power BI

## Dataset
Sales Dataset containing:
- Order ID
- Order Date
- Customer Name
- Age
- Gender
- City
- Product
- Category
- Quantity
- Unit Price
- Total Sales

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Performed descriptive analysis on the dataset:
- Summary Statistics
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Product
- Sales by Gender
- Sales by City
- Customer Age Distribution

## SQL Business Questions

Business questions answered using SQL:
- Which city generated the highest sales?
- Which product generated the highest revenue?
- Which category generated the highest sales?
- Which month recorded the highest sales?
- Which age group generated the highest sales?
- Which customers placed the highest number of orders?
- What is the gender-wise sales distribution?

## Multivariate Analysis

Performed analysis between multiple variables:
- Sales by Category and City
- Sales by Gender and Category
- Sales by Age Group
- Monthly Sales Performance

## Dashboard Mock-up

Created a Power BI dashboard including:
- Total Sales KPI
- Total Orders KPI
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Product
- Sales by Gender
- Sales by City

## Key Insights

- Electronics generated the highest revenue.
- Monthly sales fluctuated but recovered in the final months.
- Male and female sales contributions were nearly balanced.
- Electronics remained the best-performing category.
- Major sales were generated from top-performing cities.
